The Squad

Tanjiro Kamado A kind-hearted and determined boy who becomes a Demon Slayer after his family is slaughtered by demons. He seeks to cure his sister Nezuko, who was turned into a demon, while fighting to protect others.
Inosuke Hashibira A wild and aggressive Demon Slayer who wears a boar’s head mask. Raised in the mountains, he relies on his beast-like instincts and wields two serrated swords in battle. Despite his rough personality, he has a good heart.
Zenitsu Agatsuma A cowardly yet powerful Demon Slayer who constantly underestimates himself. Though he fears battle, he possesses incredible speed and strength when unconscious, using his powerful Thunder Breathing technique.
Nezuko Kamado Tanjiro’s younger sister, who was turned into a demon but retains her humanity. She is fiercely protective of her brother and allies, fighting alongside them while resisting the urge to harm humans.
